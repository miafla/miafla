Cameron

The father of the family, Ward Cameron, has three children called Rafe, Sarah and Wheezie. Rose his wife and the kids stepmother. They are Kooks and live on Figure 8. The individual family members provide plenty of action, excitement and plot twists.

Carrera

Mike, Anna and Kiara also live on Figure 8. Even though the Carreras are Kooks their daughter is a pogue at heart. Kiara's parents want to give her the best life possible but she only wants to live an adventurous life with her best friends.

Heyward

Pope and his parents live on the Cut. Pope's father owns and runs Heywards, a seafood store, which provides seafood for the entire island. Bobby, better known as Heyward, is a legendary character who can provide anything for anyone on the island.

Routledge

John B lives all by himself on the Cut because his father Big John went missing whilst searching for the long lost treasure. This is why he goes in search of the treasure and his father with his friends. John B's legal guardian is his uncle Teddy.
